## Reflection system

After **each main-quest boss**:

1. **Screen fade** (smooth transition out of combat).
2. **Show life lesson quote** (from the chapter’s quest; see [Chapters](../README.md#chapters)).
3. **Soft ambient sound** (no combat music); optional [Music](music.md) layer.
4. Player clicks **“Continue Journey”** to proceed.

Purpose: **Emotional pacing**—pause after climax, let the lesson land, then move on.

## End game

After **Chapter 5** (defeat Shadow Self, unlock [Enlightenment Mode](../chapters/chapter-5-inner-temple.md#enlightenment-mode)):

**Unlock: Path of Mastery**

- **Harder bosses** (replay or new variants).
- **New variants** (skills or enemies).
- **Cosmetic rewards** (e.g. robes, staff skins).
- **Mastery progression** (e.g. track completions, difficulty tiers).

This extends play without new story chapters; focus on challenge and collection.
