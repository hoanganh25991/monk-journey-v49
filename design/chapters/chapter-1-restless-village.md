## Narrative intro

The monk arrives at a village where the people are consumed by anger—disputes over land, old grudges, and shouting in the streets. The village elder explains that a **Rage Beast** has taken root in the heart of the settlement; it feeds on their anger and grows stronger. The monk must calm the villagers’ conflicts and then face the beast so the village can find peace.

---

## Emotional theme

- **Anger** as the central struggle: quick tempers, blame, and unresolved conflict.
- The beast is a metaphor for anger that “burns the one who carries it”—it harms the holder as much as others.
- Resolution is framed as **calm and clarity**, not victory through more anger.

---

## Gameplay objectives

1. **Explore the village** — Talk to NPCs, discover hotspots of conflict.
2. **Side objectives (optional)** — Resolve small disputes (e.g. mediate, complete short tasks).
3. **Main objective** — Clear the area of **Rage-spawned enemies** (small/elite) that embody anger.
4. **Boss objective** — Defeat the **Rage Beast** in its lair (e.g. village square or cursed shrine).

Suggested objective types from [Quest system](../systems/quest-system.md): *Kill X enemies*, *Defeat boss*, optionally *Protect NPC* or *Meditate at location*.

---

## Boss: Rage Beast

| Attribute | Notes |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | Rage Beast |
| **Visual** | Large, fire/anger-themed; chaotic, aggressive animations. |
| **Behavior** | Enrages when hit (short damage boost, telegraphed); punishes greedy DPS. Encourages calm, timed engagement. |
| **Mechanics** | Rage stacks or phases; clearing stacks or waiting for “cool-down” phases reduces danger. |
| **Reward** | 150 XP (boss), plus quest completion XP and rewards. |

Design should reinforce the lesson: rushing in with “anger” (button mashing) makes the fight harder; patience and clarity make it manageable.

---

## Reflection sequence

After the Rage Beast is defeated:

1. Screen fades (see [Reflection system](../systems/reflection-and-endgame.md)).
2. Quote displayed: **"Anger burns the one who carries it."**
3. Soft ambient music; no combat.
4. Player selects **"Continue Journey"** to proceed.

---

## Rewards

- **XP:** 150 (boss) + 100–300 (quest completion).
- **Skill point:** +1 (from main quest completion).
- **Unlock:** Access to next area (e.g. path to Forest of Doubt).
- **Optional:** Item or skill node appropriate to “Body” or “calm” theme (e.g. first Body path skill).
