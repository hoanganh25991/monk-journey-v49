## Narrative intro

The monk reaches the Inner Temple—a place of final trial. Here, the journey’s true test is not an external monster but **the self**. A **Shadow Self** appears, mirroring the monk’s own skills, stats, and choices. To pass, the monk must face this reflection and transcend the “untrained self,” earning the final skill: **Enlightenment Mode**.

---

## Emotional theme

- **Self** as the central struggle: ego, attachment to one’s build, and the idea that the biggest obstacle is our own untrained nature.
- The Shadow Self is literally “you”—same tools, same tendencies. Victory comes from mastery and growth, not from a different enemy.
- Resolution is framed as **self-mastery and enlightenment**, completing the arc from anger → fear → greed → loneliness → self.

---

## Gameplay objectives

1. **Reach the Inner Temple** — Final approach (e.g. climb, trials, or quiet path).
2. **Optional trials** — Short challenges that echo earlier themes (anger, fear, greed, loneliness) as a recap.
3. **Main objective** — Defeat the **Shadow Self** in the temple’s heart.

Objective type: *Defeat boss* (with the special rule that the boss mirrors the player).

---

## Boss: Shadow Self

| Attribute | Notes |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | Shadow Self |
| **Visual** | Dark/mirror version of the player character; same equipment and silhouette. |
| **Behavior** | **Mirrors player build:** same or scaled version of the player’s skills, stats, and possibly gear. Feels like fighting “yourself.” |
| **Mechanics** | Use the same [Skill tree](../systems/skill-tree.md) and [Progression](../systems/progression.md) data to build the Shadow’s loadout. Difficulty can scale with player level and choices. Avoid pure “copy paste” that makes the fight trivial or unfair; consider slight delays, telegraphed “ego” bursts, or phases that reward growth (e.g. using a newly learned technique). |
| **Reward** | 150 XP (boss), quest completion, and **unlock of Enlightenment Mode**. |

Design should reinforce: “Your greatest opponent is your untrained self”—the player has grown enough to overcome their own reflection.

---

## Enlightenment Mode

- **Unlock condition:** Complete Chapter 5 (defeat Shadow Self).
- **Place in [Skill tree](../systems/skill-tree.md):** Harmony path; final or capstone node.
- **Concept:** A state of peak clarity and balance—e.g. short-duration buff that enhances all paths, reduces incoming harm, or embodies “enlightenment” (e.g. no stagger, improved regen, or skill synergy).
- **Variants** (from root GDD): Increase aura size, extend duration, add healing, add slow effect.
