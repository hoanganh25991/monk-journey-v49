## Narrative intro

The monk enters a dense, misty forest where travelers have been losing their way and their courage. Villagers speak of something that “whispers in the dark” and turns confidence into doubt. The **Doubt Serpent** lurks in the heart of the forest, feeding on fear. The monk must move through the uncertain paths and face the serpent so that the forest—and those who pass through it—can be free of paralyzing fear.

---

## Emotional theme

- **Fear** as the central struggle: hesitation, avoidance, and the way fear grows when left unspoken and unexamined.
- The serpent represents doubt that coils around the mind; confronting it (literally and emotionally) is the path through.
- Resolution is framed as **courage through action and clarity**, not by staying silent.

---

## Gameplay objectives

1. **Explore the forest** — Navigate misty, winding paths; discover lost travelers or waypoints.
2. **Side objectives** — Help NPCs overcome fear (e.g. escort, light torches, find lost items).
3. **Main objective** — Encounter and clear **Doubt-spawned enemies** (e.g. phantoms, shadows) that sap confidence or cause disorientation.
4. **Boss objective** — Defeat the **Doubt Serpent** in its lair (e.g. clearing at the forest’s heart).

Suggested objective types: *Kill X enemies*, *Defeat boss*, *Protect NPC*, *Meditate at location*, or *Survive waves* in a “doubt” wave event.

---

## Boss: Doubt Serpent

| Attribute | Notes |
|-----------|--------|
| **Name** | Doubt Serpent |
| **Visual** | Serpentine, shadow/mist-themed; elusive, coiling movements. |
| **Behavior** | Hides in mist or darkness; strikes from uncertainty. May apply “doubt” debuffs (e.g. reduced accuracy, vision, or delayed actions). |
| **Mechanics** | Visibility or “certainty” mechanics (e.g. light sources, standing in clear zones) reduce its power or reveal its weak phase. |
| **Reward** | 150 XP (boss), plus quest completion. |

Design should reinforce the lesson: fear grows when we stay passive; moving forward and “shedding light” on doubt weakens it.

---

## Reflection sequence

After the Doubt Serpent is defeated:

1. Screen fades.
2. Quote displayed: **"Fear grows in silence."**
3. Soft ambient; player clicks **"Continue Journey"**.
